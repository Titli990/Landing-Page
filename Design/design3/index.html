Frequently-asked questions

What is the expected timeline for completion? −

We are strictly on track for March 2026. Currently, we are finalizing the landscape and common area tiling. Quality checks are performed at every milestone.

Is the project fully approved? +

Yes, the project is 100% compliant with MDDA and RERA. We provide all transparency certificates during your site visit for absolute peace of mind.

Which banks are providing home loans? +

We have established partnerships with Nationalised Banks Bank of Maharashtra and The Nainital Bank. Our dedicated finance desk helps you with documentation and fast-track approvals.

What are the key material specifications? +

Each unit features premium modular kitchens, designer vitrified flooring, and high-durability fixtures that blend luxury with longevity.

What makes this location premium? +

Proximity is key. You are within 10 minutes of the city's best education hubs, healthcare centers, and shopping malls.

Is interior customization possible? +

We offer a "Personal Touch" program where homeowners can choose specific wall finishes and layout tweaks during the early construction phase.
